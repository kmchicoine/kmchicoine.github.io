PCT 2020

2600 miles, 5 months, 1 adventure

Sometimes, life pushes you in strange directions. In my case, that direction is north, from the Mexican border to the Canadian border through the Sierra-Nevada and Cascade mountain ranges. What makes someone voluntarily hike across the country? For me, it's a special cocktail that's 3 parts wanderlust and 2 parts youthful exuberance, with a dash of quarter-life crisis and genetic predisposition to outdoor exploits, served lit with the flame of grief. So, as the oft-used John Muir quote goes: the mountains are calling and I must go.
