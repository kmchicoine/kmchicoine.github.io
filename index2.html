About Me

About

I'm a software developer currently living in Bend, OR! My work thus far has focused on systems engineering and backend development, with recent experience building web APIs and adapting data structures. I enjoy exploration and learning new skills, both professionally and in my personal life.

When not at my computer, you can find me in the mountains. I hike, bike, climb, and ski, with a special love for multi-day, off trail adventures. On foot, I have hiked the Southern Sierra High Route, Skurka's Wind River High Route, and created my own high route in the Trinity Alps. On my bike, I've ridden the Arizona Trail 300, visited all of Utah's National Parks, and rode RAGBRAI L. I frequently plan trips for my friends and have organized groups of 12 on backpacking and hut ski trips.

I occasionally write about my outdoor pursuits here. You can find more pictures on my Instagram page and more activities by checking out my Strava profile.
